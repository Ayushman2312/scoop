Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a landing page?

A basic landing page can be built in 2-4 hours using page builders, while a more complex, custom design may take 1-2 days depending on your experience level and requirements. Planning and content creation typically take longer than the actual building process.

Do I need coding skills to create a landing page?

No, you can create effective landing pages using drag-and-drop builders like WordPress with Elementor, Unbounce, or Leadpages. However, basic HTML/CSS knowledge can be helpful for advanced customization and troubleshooting.

What's the ideal length for a landing page?

Landing page length should match your audience's needs and the complexity of your offer. Simple offers (like email signups) may work with shorter pages, while complex products or services often require longer, more detailed pages to address all concerns and objections.

How do I measure landing page success?

Key metrics include conversion rate (most important), bounce rate, time on page, and goal completions. Use Google Analytics to track these metrics and identify optimization opportunities. A good conversion rate varies by industry but typically ranges from 2-5% for most businesses.
